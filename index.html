How It Works

1. Set Your Time

Tap buttons or use keyboard ('m'/'s') to dial in minutes and seconds.

2. Start and Monitor

Hit spacebar or button. Watch countdown in dock, full screen, or mini mode.

3. Alert and Review

Ring sounds, display flashes on expiry. See elapsed time instantly.

4. Reset and Repeat

Press '0' to zero out. Ready for the next round.

Absolutely—spacebar starts/stops, 'm' adds minutes, 's' adds seconds, '0' resets.

It counts up from zero if started there, or alerts precisely on expiry.
